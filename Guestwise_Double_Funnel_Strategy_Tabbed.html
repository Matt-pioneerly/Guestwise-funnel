ABM Engine

Systematic approach to engaging all 100 target accounts

üéØ Account-Based Marketing

PRIMARY STRATEGY

The ABM engine is the core of this strategy‚Äîsystematically working through every target account with personalized, multi-touch campaigns coordinated across LinkedIn, email, events, and direct outreach.

üîç Account Selection & Research
  • Finalize list of 100 target accounts with website URLs
  • Research key decision-makers via LinkedIn (CMO, Finance Director, Commercial Director)
  • Enrich data using Clay (company news, tech stack, recent funding)
  • Segment by priority: Tier 1 (20 accounts), Tier 2 (30 accounts), Tier 3 (50 accounts)
  • Identify current pain points and triggers (hiring freeze, expansion news)
üî• Account Warming (Weeks 1-4)
  • Thought Leader Ads target decision-makers at all 100 accounts
  • Founders engage with prospects' LinkedIn content organically
  • Create "surround sound"‚Äîprospects see Guestwise everywhere
  • Track website visits from target accounts with retargeting pixels
  • Goal: 70%+ of target accounts have seen content before outreach begins
üì® Multi-Touch Outreach (Weeks 5-8)
  • Personalized LinkedIn connection requests from founders (reference company-specific insights)
  • Follow-up DMs share relevant content (case studies, ROI calculator)
  • 3-4 touch email campaigns using Clay automation
  • Thought Leader Ads continue throughout sequences for consistent visibility
  • Phone outreach added for Tier 1 accounts
ü§ù High-Touch Engagement (Weeks 9-12)
  • Executive events: Industry dinners at high-end venues
  • Invitation-only CMO peer roundtables on key topics
  • Personalized video messages from founders to engaged accounts
  • Physical direct mail: Holiday Marketing Playbook sent to top 20 accounts
  • Account-specific LinkedIn video ad campaigns for engaged prospects
üíé Demo & Proof of Value
  • Personalized demos showing solutions to their specific data fragmentation challenges
  • Free marketing efficiency audits for serious prospects
  • Customer introductions (Mitchell & Butler) for reference calls
  • Custom ROI modeling showing cost replacement vs. hiring a marketing executive
üèÜ Close & Expand
  • Pilot programs starting with 5-10 locations to prove value
  • Finance Director approval process focused on ROI
  • Phased rollout: Web Optimization ‚Üí Email/Social ‚Üí Full Platform
  • Success tracking and documentation for future case studies
üîÑ Continuous Nurture
  • Not ready yet? Add to monthly newsletter for ongoing engagement
  • Quarterly re-engagement campaigns with new content and offers
  • Ongoing event invitations to maintain relationships
  • Patience for 3-6 month sales cycles tied to budget years
